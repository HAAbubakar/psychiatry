Elle Pierre

Elle Pierre is a board certified psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner (PMHNP-BC) who assesses and treats adults and adolescents with depression, anxiety disorders, mood disorders, psychotic disorders, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), and substance use disorders. She has experience in both inpatient and outpatient care. Elle received her training at Grand Canyon University, earning a Bachelor of Science in Nursing. She went on for her Master of Science in Nursing from Walden University and received her Certificate as a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner. Prior to joining National Psychiatry LLC, she was a nurse in Naples Community Hospital and Gulf Coast Medical Center. She is a member of the American Psychiatric Nurses Association and is fluent in Creole and French.

Pam Kain

Dr Kain graduated magna cum laude from California State University Long Beach with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology. She then attended APA accredited California School of Professional Psychology Los Angeles, and received a Masters degree and a Doctor of Psychology degree. Her specialty was Multicultural and Community Psychology in the Clinical Psychology program. Dr Kain has worked extensively in outpatient and inpatient psychiatric facilities as well as inpatient medical settings as a consultant. Compliance with the Baker Act law, assessing patients placed under a Baker Act, and Mental Capacity evaluations are areas of focus and specialization developed by Dr Kain at these facilities. She has maintained a small private practice specializing in childhood trauma and mood disorders relating to trauma throughout most of her career. Dr Kain enjoys listening to jazz, playing with her 3 cats, and visiting her 2 adorable young grandsons in California when she is not working.

Ryan Hughes

Ryan worked as an RN at an inpatient geropsychiatry unit in his hometown, Knoxville, TN. Shortly after, he decided to go back to school to become a practitioner to increasingly optimize the health of his patients. Ryan chose the field of psychiatry due to the ability to truly get to know patients and understand them in a unique way. He finds value in connecting and listening to patients. Making a difference and providing mental clarity to patients are what matters most to him. His never-ending goal is to provide the simplest, most natural, and effective plan for patients to reach their highest potential. In his spare time, Ryan loves to learn, play sports such as tennis and jiu jitsu, walk his golden retriever Sully, cook, do anything outside, and spend time with family.

Watson Camilus

Watson Camilus is a board-certified psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner (PMHNP-BC). He is passionate about providing quality, compassionate, and comprehensive mental health services to pediatrics, adolescents, and adults. Watson possesses strong clinical skills enhanced by his natural ability to build rapport with patients and families. He follows evidence-based guidelines blended with clinical experience, personal observation, and intuition. He completed his Bachelor of Science in Nursing at Western Governors University in Salt Lake City, Utah, and then earned his Master of Science degree at Walden University in Minneapolis, Minnesota. He holds a national certification with the American Nurses Credentialing Center (ANCC) as a psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner.
